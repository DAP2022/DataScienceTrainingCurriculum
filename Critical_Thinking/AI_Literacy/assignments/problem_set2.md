**Day 2: Diagnosing AI Behavior** 

**Motivation:** _Answering the question: why does this tool behave this way?_


**Activity:** Return to your Ai Use Case from yesterday. Find one example in your chosen workflow, where Ai:
  -  produced a strong result, and
  -  produced a weak, incorrect, or misleading result. 

  For each example:
  1.	Describe the behavior. 
  2.	Indentify which Ai architecture discussed in class might contribute to this behavior? 
  3.	What data, training process, or design choice might explain the outcome? 
  4.	What could a developer or user do to improve performance? 
  5.	Which explanation seems most likely: limitations of the model architecture, limitations of the training data, limitations of the prompt, or limitations of the task itself?

**Learning goal** Move from "the Ai made a mistake" to "why this system behaves this way."

**Deliverable** 1 page.
