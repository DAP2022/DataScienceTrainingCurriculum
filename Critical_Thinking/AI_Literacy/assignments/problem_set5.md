**Day 5: Personal Ai Fluency Protocol** 

**Motivation:** Answering the question: how can I use Ai responsibly?

**Activity:** Create a personal AI Fluency Protocol for your work or field. Your protocol should include:
1. **Delegation Map**. What tasks should be:
- Fully human 
- Human-led with AI assistance 
- Ai-led with human review 
- Fully automated (if appropriate) 

2. **Risk Assessment**: Using either the Belmont or FAT framework: Identify at least **three risks** associated with your Ai use.
Examples: bias, privacy, hallucination, overreliance, transparency, accountability 

3. **Verification Strategy**: How will you:
- verify outputs?
- document Ai use?
- identify when Ai should not be used? 

4. **Future Use**: Describe one concrete way you plan to incorporate Ai into your workflow over the next six months.

5. What is one belief you held about AI at the beginning of this course that has changed?

**Learning goal** Demonstrate that you have a clear framework for your interactions with Ai.

**Deliverable** 1-2 page protocol.
